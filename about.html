About Us

At RainyDays, we’re more than just an online shop. We’re a lifestyle brand dedicated to pushing your comfort zone. We understand that life’s most memorable experiences often happen in the great outdoors, and we’re here to ensure you can embrace every moment, no matter the weather.

Our Jurney

Our journey began with a vision: to create rain jackets that don’t just protect you from the elements but em-power you to thrive in the elements. We believe that rainy days should never limit your adventures. Instead, they should become opportunities to explore, connect, and enjoy life to the fullest. Established in 1995, RainyDays has been on a mission to redefine the way people experience outdoor activ-ities. Our unisex rain jackets are thoughtfully de-signed to cater to diverse range of individuals who share our passion for adventure.
